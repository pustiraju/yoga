Welcome to Kid Yoga

Join Yoga Samadhan’s YCB-approved yoga program for Classes 1-8 students, bringing fitness, focus, and fun to young minds, authorized by the Ministry of AYUSH, India

Why Yoga for Kids?

Boosts Focus

Yoga helps kids concentrate better in class with mindfulness and breathing exercises.

Builds Strength

Fun poses like Tree Pose improve balance, flexibility, and physical fitness.

Reduces Stress

Simple meditation techniques calm young minds, promoting emotional well-being.

Enroll in Our Kids’ Yoga Program

Yoga teachers, event organizers, and schools can bring our YCB-approved yoga program to Class 1-8 students. Partner with Yoga Samadhan to promote wellness in young minds!

Enrollment Details

For Yoga Teachers:

  • Yoga instructors or Teachers can enroll their students in our program.
  • Partner with us to teach the 30-hour program in schools or community centers.
  • Receive training materials and support to deliver the CYP-based curriculum.
  • Contact us at suman.gbn123@gmail.com to collaborate.

For Event Organizers:

  • Organize yoga workshops, camps, or events for Class 1-8 students using our program.
  • Ideal for NGOs, community groups, or summer camps.
  • We provide curriculum, certificates, and instructor support (if needed).
  • Email suman.gbn123@gmail.com to plan your event.

For Schools:

  • Enroll your Class 1-8 students in our program, integrated into PE classes or extracurricular activities.
  • Flexible scheduling: in-school sessions or online via Zoom.
  • Contact suman.gbn123@gmail.com to discuss partnerships.
