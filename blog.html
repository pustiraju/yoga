What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that originated in India over 5,000 years ago. The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word yuj, which means "to unite" or "to join." It symbolizes the union of the individual self with the universal consciousness. Yoga combines physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), meditation, and ethical practices to promote physical health, mental clarity, emotional stability, and spiritual growth. It is widely recognized not only as a form of exercise but also as a tool for enhancing overall wellbeing.
